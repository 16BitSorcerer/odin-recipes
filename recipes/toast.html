Avo Toastie

Avocado toast, the epitome of trendy, socially conscious dining, is a delectable masterpiece that embodies the intersection of veganism, CrossFit, and self-actualization. Crafted with care by young, hip, and impressionable recent college graduates who are passionate about social justice, this dish symbolizes their journey towards enlightenment and healthier living. By smashing ripe avocados onto artisanal whole-grain bread and sprinkling it with a touch of pink Himalayan salt and freshly ground black pepper, they create a culinary marvel that not only nourishes the body but also aligns with their beliefs in sustainable agriculture and animal rights. With each bite of creamy avocado goodness, they feel empowered, knowing they are contributing to a better world while also showcasing their dedication to trendy food culture.

Ingredients

  • Two ethically sourced slices of whole bread
  • An ethically sustainable sourced ripe avocado
  • A pinch of Himalayan pink salt from child-labour-free sources
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare Avocado Spread: Mash avocado with pink salt and pepper for balance.
  2. Toast Bread: Toast whole-grain slices until golden.
  3. Spread and Garnish: Spread avocado mix on toast, optionally garnish with microgreens or tomatoes.
  4. Serve Mindfully: Present and enjoy each bite, mindful of ethical and social values.
